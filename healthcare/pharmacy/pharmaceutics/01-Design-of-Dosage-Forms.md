## 1 Concepts

- [ ] Pharmaceutics is the science of preparation of drugs, dosage forms, and
      drug delivery systems, taking into account the **pharmacokinetics** and
      **pharmacodynamics** of the drug as well as its physical and chemical
      properties.

- [ ] The **excipients** (塑型剂) provide varied and specialized pharmaceutical
      functions as a **formulation additives** (复方添加剂):

  - **solubilize** (溶解)
  - **suspend** (悬浮)
  - **thicken** (增稠)
  - **preserve** (防腐)
  - **emulsify** (乳化)
  - modify **dissolution** (溶出速率)
  - increase the **compactability** (增加压片的紧实性)
  - improve the flavour of drug substances

- [ ] Principle of dosage form design

  - physical and chemical stability
  - suitable preservation against microbial **contamination** (微生物污染)
  - uniformity of the dose of the drug
  - acceptability to users, both **prescriber** (开处方的人)
  - suitable packaging and labeling
  - independent of patient-to-patient variation

## 2 Mission

- [ ] Basic theories

  - **rheology** (流变学, 液体、半固体制剂的黏稠度与流动性), **micromeritics** (粉体学,
    研究药物粉末/颗粒的物理性质).
  - dissolution and solubility
  - surface and interfacial phenomena (表面与界面现象)
  - **disperse system** (分散体系)
  - tablet formation
  - kinetics of drug release
  - kinetics of product stability
  - intelligent release
  - biopharmaceutics

- [ ] Technologies

  - **micronization** (微粒化)
