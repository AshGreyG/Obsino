## 1 Sterilization Concepts

+ **sterile preparations** (无菌制剂)
  + sterilized preparations (灭菌制剂)
  + aseptic preparations (无菌制剂)
  + destruction or removal of microorganisms (**vegetative cell**, 繁殖体 and
    **endospore**, 芽孢)

## 2 Sterilization Processes

+ physical sterilization
  + dry heat
    + flame
    + dry heat air
  + **moist** (潮湿的) heat / steam
    + heat pressure
    + flowing steam
    + boiling
    + **fractional sterilization** (间歇灭菌法) at low temperature
  + ray
    + **radiation** (辐射放射线)
    + uv
    + micro wave
  + **filtration** (过滤, 滤清)
+ chemical sterilization
  + gaseous
  + **chemical reagent solution** (化学试剂溶液)
+ aseptic processing

+ **D value** (number vs time or dose). The time or radiation dose taken for a
  one log reduction (90%) in the number of microorganisms at certain temperature.

  $$
    \frac{\mathrm{d}N}{\mathrm{d}t} = -kN
  $$
