- Mass Spectrometry (质谱)
  - Gas Chromatography (GC-MS, 气相色谱-质谱联用技术)
  - Liquid Chromatography (LC-MS, 液相色谱-质谱联用技术)
  - For proteomics (蛋白质组学)

- How to **ionisation**
  - **Electrospray Ionisation** (ESI, 电喷雾电离)
    - High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC, 高效液相色谱法) \
    => High positive potential applied to **capillary needle** (毛细管针). $e^-$
    to capillary needle. \
    => $e^+$ **charged droplets spray** (带电液滴喷雾) -> **coulomb fission**
    (库仑爆炸) -> gas phase ions \
    => $e^-$ applied to **capillary inlet** (毛细管) to mass spectrometer.
  - **Electron Impact Ionisation** (EI, 电子轰击电离)
  - **Matrix Assisted Laser Desorption Ionisation** (MALDI, 基质辅助激光解吸电离)
