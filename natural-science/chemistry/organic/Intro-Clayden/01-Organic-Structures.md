## 1 Functional Groups

+ [o] Bubble ethane gas through **acids** (酸), **bases** (碱), **oxidizing
  agents** (氧化剂) or **reducing agents** (还原剂), it will remain unchanged.
+ [o] The oxidation (氧化) of ethanol by microorganisms (微生物) that grow in wine
  left open to the air leads to acetic acid / ethanoic acid (乙酸) while the
  oxidation of ethanol by the **liver** (肝脏) gives acetaldehyde / ethanal (乙醛).

  ``` smiles
  CCO>>CC(=O)O __{'textAboveArrow': 'liver'}__,
  CCO>>CC(=O)  __{'textAboveArrow': 'microorganism'}__
  ```

  functional groups are very important in organic reactions.
+ [o] The human metabolism makes use of the oxidation of alcohols to render
  harmless other toxic compounds containing the $\text{OH}$ group. Lactic acid
  (乳酸) produced in muscles during intense activities, is oxidized by an enzyme
  called **lactate dehydrogenase** (乳酸脱氢酶) to the metabolically useful
  compound **pyruvic acid** (丙酮酸).

  ``` smiles
  CC(O)C(=O)O>>CC(=O)C(=O)O __{'textAboveArrow': 'lactate dehydrogenase'}__
  ```

+ [o] Functional groups:
  + **Alkanes** (烷烃) don't have any functional groups;
  + **Alkenes** / **Olefins** (烯烃) contain $\text{C=C}$ double bonds (some of
    the compounds produced by plants and used by perfumers are alkenes like
    $\alpha$-pinene ($\alpha$-蒎烯), limonene (柠檬烯));
  + **Alkynes** (炔烃) contain $\text{C}\equiv\text{C}$ triple bonds;
  + **Alcohols** (醇) contain a **hydroxyl** group (羟基) (carbohydrates are
    peppered with hydroxyl groups, like sucrose (蔗糖)). **The liver carries
    out its tasks of detoxifying (排毒) unwanted organic compounds by repeatedly
    hydroxylating (羟基化) them until they are water soluble, and they are then
    excreted in the bile (胆汁) or urine (尿液)**;
  + **Ethers** (醚) contain an **alkoxy group** (烷氧基). Brevetoxin B (短裸甲藻
    毒素 B), the many ether oxygen interfere with sodium ion metabolism;
  + **Amines** (胺) contain the **amino group** (氨基);
  + **Nitro** compounds (硝基化合物) contain the nitro group. Notice nitro group
    cannot have five bonds, its structure is;

    ``` smiles
    C[N+](=O)[O-]
    ```

  + **Alkyl halides** (卤代烃): **fluorides** (氟代烃) - fluoro (氟原子官能团), 
    **chlorides** (氯代烃) - chloro (氯原子官能团), **bromides** (溴代烃) - bromo (
    溴原子官能团), **iodides** (碘代烃) - iodo (碘原子官能团);
  + **Aldehydes** (醛) and **ketones** (酮) contain the **carbonyl group** (羰基);
  + **Carboxylic acids** (羧酸) contain the **carboxyl group** (羧基) (for example
    citric acid (柠檬酸), malic acid (苹果酸), tartaric acid (酒石酸));
  + **Esters** (酯) contain **a carboxyl group with an extra alkyl group** (带
    烷基或者其衍生物的羧基称为酯基). Fats are esters, they are formed in the body by
    condensing **glycerol** (甘油);
